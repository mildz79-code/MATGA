## 📋 About

MATGA is a national initiative to restore domestic ring-spun yarn manufacturing in the United States. The project addresses a critical gap in the American textile supply chain: while the U.S. grows some of the world's finest premium cotton (Pima/Supima), there are **zero ring-spun yarn mills** in the entire western United States. As a result, 90% of premium cotton fiber is exported raw — unspun — costing American farmers and the broader economy billions.

### Key Facts

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| US premium cotton exported raw | **90%** |
| Ring-spun yarn mills in western US | **Zero** |
| Rise in US farm bankruptcies (H1 2025) | **57%** |
| Wasted per lb in overseas logistics | **$1.50+** |

### The Western Spin Initiative

The centerpiece project is a proposed ring-spinning mill in **Yuma, Arizona** — strategically located near premium cotton farms — to process domestic fiber into high-quality yarn for American textile manufacturers, with a focus on the **LA 2028 Olympics** supply chain opportunity.
