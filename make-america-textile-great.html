The Dual Crisis

U.S. Cotton Farmers Are Being Squeezed Out of Their Own Market

American cotton farmers grow some of the world's finest fiber ‚Äî including Supima and Pima cotton found in luxury dress shirts worldwide. Farmers see none of that value.

The human toll is devastating: agriculture worker suicides are running at three times the national average. The system is broken ‚Äî and it doesn't have to be.
